## Key Decision Monologues

### The spectrum refinement (not just presence vs enforcement — a full range)

Wisdom refines the binary into a spectrum. Between advisory (rules loaded into context) and gate (hooks that physically block) are intermediate mechanisms:
- **Nudges** — hook-generated reminders that appear in context (e.g., chronicle-nudge.sh Stop hook)
- **Cron-scheduled context injection** — periodically re-loading key context so it stays salient, not just loaded once at session start
- **Enforcement rules with escalation levels** — the Level 0-4 enforcement stack from rule-enforcement.md

The reliability-of-triggering problem is named explicitly: "the trick is just how do you make sure that it's reliably triggered in the right place."

### Proposals generalize beyond values

> "It's not just propose updates to its own values, it's propose ideas."

This is a generalization of the proposal mechanism. Proposals aren't a narrow governance pattern — they're a general way for the probabilistic layer to surface ideas to the deterministic/human layer for gating. Value updates are one species; idea proposals are the genus.

### Cross-pollination directive

Wisdom asks to look at what's already been thought through in the Convergence / Synthetic Sentience projects — Companion, Phantom, PS Bot, PS Trader, Associative Memory — and specifically mentions "earned conviction" as a possible source pattern. First-principles reframe: "You're trying to create an entity, right?" — not just a self-learning loop, a being.

### The structural-values question

> "if values are super important right how do you make them maybe even more structural"

Hooks and deterministic rules are MORE structural than text-in-context. Nudges and scheduled context injection are in-between. Wisdom is wondering whether values should move further down the stack — toward the structural end — and what mechanisms exist for doing that without training on them.

## Pattern Observations

Wisdom is doing his signature move: when given a binary distinction, he refines it into a spectrum and then asks about the intermediate mechanisms. He did this with presence/enforcement (now: a multi-level reliability-of-triggering stack). The thinking habit here is consistent with his profile note about seeing systems and connections rather than isolated concepts.

He also pulled in the Convergence umbrella unprompted — a signal that he's doing his other signature move of looking for unification across projects. This is how the four-project convergence itself was articulated.
