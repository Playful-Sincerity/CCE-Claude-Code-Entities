**The line:** You're already in Claude Code. Run a skill. Now that conversation has identity, continuity, and a heartbeat. No separate application. No deployment. No bridge layer. The conversation you were already having becomes a persistent entity.

**Key language:**

- "Turn any Claude Code conversation into an entity with identity, initiative, and a heartbeat."
- "No separate codebase. No deployment. The entity IS the conversation plus a few markdown files."
- "Run one command. Now you have a calendar assistant with a heartbeat."
- "Same infrastructure, different tuning. A thinking partner for you, an on-call assistant for your client."
- "The conversation you were already having becomes a persistent entity that keeps running."

**For HHA:** The barrier to entry is basically zero. Open Claude Code, run one command, now you have a personal assistant that knows your calendar, your energy patterns, and your morning practice.

**For Hearth / Good Drivers:** This is the natural evolution of Claude Code power use. You've built skills, rules, hooks. Now your system has a heartbeat.
